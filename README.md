A curated collection of resources for undergraduate and graduate-level mathematics and statistics. This repository aims to provide a roadmap of standard "Bible" textbooks, online courses, and tools for mastering the mathematical sciences.

## Structure

- **[RESOURCES.md](./RESOURCES.md)**: The core list of textbooks, video lectures, and tools, categorized by academic level and subject.

## Key Areas Covered

### Undergraduate
- **Calculus**: The study of change and motion.
- **Linear Algebra**: The study of vectors, spaces, and linear mappings.
- **Real Analysis**: Rigorous theoretical foundations of calculus.
- **Abstract Algebra**: Study of algebraic structures (groups, rings, fields).
- **Probability & Statistics**: Analysis of random phenomena and data.

### Graduate
- **Measure Theory**: Generalization of integration and length.
- **Functional Analysis**: Study of vector spaces endowed with limit-related structures.
- **Topology**: Properties of space preserved under continuous deformation.
- **Advanced Statistics**: Stochastic processes, Bayesian inference, and statistical theory.
