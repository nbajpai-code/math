# Mathematics & Statistics Resources

## üéì Undergraduate Mathematics

### Calculus
- **[Spivak, *Calculus*](https://www.amazon.com/Calculus-4th-Michael-Spivak/dp/0914098918)**: Rigorous, theoretical approach. The "gold standard" for math majors.
- **[Stewart, *Calculus*](https://www.amazon.com/Calculus-Early-Transcendentals-James-Stewart/dp/1285741552)**: Standard computational text used in most universities.

### Linear Algebra
- **[Axler, *Linear Algebra Done Right*](https://linear.axler.net/)**: Focuses on operators and vector spaces; famous for avoiding determinants initially.
- **[Strang, *Introduction to Linear Algebra*](https://math.mit.edu/~gs/linearalgebra/)**: Accompanying text for his famous MIT OCW course.
- **[Lay, *Linear Algebra and Its Applications*](https://www.pearson.com/en-us/subject-catalog/p/linear-algebra-and-its-applications/P200000006338)**: Good standard introduction.

### Real Analysis
- **[Rudin, *Principles of Mathematical Analysis* ("Baby Rudin")](https://www.amazon.com/Principles-Mathematical-Analysis-International-Mathematics/dp/007054235X)**: The classic, rigorous text. Notoriously concise.
- **[Abbott, *Understanding Analysis*](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-1-4939-2712-8)**: A more accessible and intuitive introduction than Rudin.

### Abstract Algebra
- **[Dummit & Foote, *Abstract Algebra*](https://www.amazon.com/Abstract-Algebra-3rd-David-Dummit/dp/0471433349)**: Comprehensive standard reference.
- **[Pinter, *A Book of Abstract Algebra*](https://store.doverpublications.com/products/9780486474175)**: Excellent, affordable (Dover) introductory text.

### Differential Equations
- **[Boyce & DiPrima, *Elementary Differential Equations*](https://www.wiley.com/en-us/Elementary+Differential+Equations+and+Boundary+Value+Problems%2C+11th+Edition-p-9781119320630)**: Standard undergraduate text.
- **[3Blue1Brown (Differential Equations Playlist)](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDNPOjrT6KVlfJuKtYTftqH6)**: Beautiful visual intuition.

## üéì Graduate Mathematics

### Analysis (Real & Complex)
- **[Rudin, *Real and Complex Analysis* ("Papa Rudin")](https://www.amazon.com/Real-Complex-Analysis-Higher-Mathematics/dp/0070542341)**: Standard graduate text combining real and complex analysis.
- **[Folland, *Real Analysis*](https://www.amazon.com/Real-Analysis-Modern-Techniques-Applications/dp/0471317160)**: Modern treatment covering measure theory and functional analysis.
- **[Ahlfors, *Complex Analysis*](https://www.amazon.com/Complex-Analysis-McGraw-Hill-International-Editions/dp/0070006571)**: Classic reference.

### Algebra
- **[Lang, *Algebra*](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-1-4613-0041-0)**: Encyclopedic and rigorous.
- **[Hungerford, *Algebra*](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-1-4612-6101-8)**: Another standard graduate reference.

### Topology & Geometry
- **[Munkres, *Topology*](https://www.amazon.com/Topology-2nd-James-Munkres/dp/0131816292)**: The standard for point-set and introductory algebraic topology.
- **[Hatcher, *Algebraic Topology*](https://pi.math.cornell.edu/~hatcher/AT/AT.html)**: Free online; standard for algebraic topology.
- **[Tu, *An Introduction to Manifolds*](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-1-4419-7400-6)**: Great entry point for differential geometry.

## üìä Statistics (Undergraduate & Graduate)

### Undergraduate
- **[Wackerly, Mendenhall, Scheaffer, *Mathematical Statistics with Applications*](https://www.cengage.com/c/mathematical-statistics-with-applications-7e-wackerly/9780495110811/)**: Solid calculus-based introduction.
- **[Ross, *A First Course in Probability*](https://www.pearson.com/en-us/subject-catalog/p/first-course-in-probability-a/P200000006326)**: Standard text for probability theory.

### Graduate
- **[Casella & Berger, *Statistical Inference*](https://www.amazon.com/Statistical-Inference-George-Casella/dp/0534243126)**: The absolute "Bible" for first-year graduate statistics theory.
- **[Lehmann & Casella, *Theory of Point Estimation*](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-0-387-98502-2)**: Advanced theoretical statistic.
- **[Gelman et al., *Bayesian Data Analysis*](http://www.stat.columbia.edu/~gelman/book/)**: The standard text for Bayesian statistics.

## üèõÔ∏è Open Access Class Notes & Lecture Notes (.edu)
High-quality, free resources from top universities and mathematicians.

- **[Paul's Online Math Notes (Lamar University)](https://tutorial.math.lamar.edu/)**: The "gold standard" for clear, accessible tutorials on Calculus (I, II, III) and Differential Equations.
- **[Terence Tao's Blog (UCLA)](https://terrytao.wordpress.com/books/)**: Lecture notes on Real Analysis, Measure Theory, and Linear Algebra from a Fields Medalist.
- **[The Rising Sea (Ravi Vakil, Stanford)](https://math.stanford.edu/~vakil/216blog/)**: Comprehensive, famous notes for the *Foundations of Algebraic Geometry*.
- **[An Infinitely Large Napkin (Evan Chen, MIT)](https://web.evanchen.cc/napkin.html)**: A massive, high-level overview of advanced mathematics (from Groups to Algebraic Topology) written for undergraduates.
- **[MIT OpenCourseWare (OCW)](https://ocw.mit.edu/search/?t=Mathematics)**: Full course materials (exams, lecture notes, videos) for the entire MIT mathematics curriculum.
- **[Chicago Undergraduate Mathematics Bibliography](https://github.com/sgerhardt/chicagomath)**: A famous list of book recommendations and notes from the University of Chicago mathematics department.

## üíª GitHub Repositories
Curated lists and tools hosted on GitHub.

- **[awesome-math](https://github.com/rossant/awesome-math)**: The definitive "awesome" list for mathematics. Contains links to hundreds of books, courses, and tools.
- **[manim](https://github.com/3b1b/manim)**: The animation engine used by 3Blue1Brown. Great for programmers who want to create mathematical visualizations.
- **[Open Access Texts](https://openaccesstexts.com/)**: A directory of open-access mathematics textbooks.
- **[Lean Theorem Prover](https://github.com/leanprover/lean4)**: Resources for the Lean theorem prover, widely used in the digitization of mathematics.
